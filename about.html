About Gorilla Business Group

Gorilla Business Group is a South African family-owned industrial and construction business focused on resilient, locally-made concrete products and practical construction services. Founded with the aim of improving housing delivery and infrastructure resilience in peri-urban communities, we combine modern manufacturing with hands-on construction know-how.

Mission

To manufacture and supply reliable, cost-effective concrete products and provide construction services that meet SANS standards, support local employment, and deliver value to homeowners, contractors and municipalities across Gauteng and Mpumalanga.

Values

  • Quality: SANS-compliant manufacturing and consistent testing.
  • Local impact: Job creation, skills transfer and ethical procurement.
  • Reliability: On-time deliveries and clear lead times.
  • Safety & Compliance: Health & safety and environmental awareness.

Who we serve

Homeowners, small-to-medium developers, landscapers, municipalities and commercial clients requiring bulk deliveries or turnkey installations.
